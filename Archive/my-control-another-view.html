Intro & Guidance

My Controls

The Master Controls dashboard provides a register of all control tests currently requiring assesment or review that are applicable to you.

This is a component of the Compliance Management solution.

The reports on this dashboard will be filtered to show only content specific to you.

If you require further assistance please raise a new support ticket through the information menu (i) at the top right of the screen.
